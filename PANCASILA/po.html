Cyber Bullying Awareness

Raising awareness and offering help to those affected by cyber bullying.

Apa itu Cyber Bullying?

Cyber bullying is bullying that takes place over digital devices like phones, computers, or tablets. It includes harmful actions like spreading rumors, sending threatening messages, and excluding someone from online groups.

How to Prevent Cyber Bullying

  • Keep personal information private
  • Be respectful to others online
  • Report inappropriate behavior
  • Support victims and encourage empathy

If You're a Victim, Get Help

If you or someone you know is being cyberbullied, it's important to talk to a trusted adult or counselor. Here are some helpful resources:

  • StopBullying.gov
  • Cyberbullying.org
